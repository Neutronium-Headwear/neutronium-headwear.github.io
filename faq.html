Orders and Shipping

Can I modify my order after it has been placed?

Once an order has been placed, it is immediately processed to ensure that you receive your items as quickly as possible. As such, we cannot guarantee that modifications can be made. If you need to make changes, please contact us as soon as possible, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our shipping provider's website to track the status of your delivery.

Product Information

How do I choose the right hat size?

To choose the correct hat size, you can measure the circumference of your head where the hat will rest, usually about an inch above your eyebrows. Compare this measurement to the size guide provided on our product pages.

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are securely processed.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, your personal information is protected with industry-standard encryption technology. We take your security very seriously and ensure that our systems are up-to-date with the latest security measures.
